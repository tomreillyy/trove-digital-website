HPS Recruitment

A professional website for a Perth labour hire and recruitment agency specializing in blue-collar industries.

Services

Web Design · Development

Timeline

Overview

HPS Recruitment is a Perth-based labour hire and recruitment agency specializing in blue-collar workers across mining, construction, civil, and industrial sectors. They came to us wanting a professional online presence that would attract both employers looking for staff and candidates seeking work opportunities.

The challenge was to create a clear website that served two distinct audiences while showcasing their expertise and transparent pricing.

The Problem

HPS Recruitment needed to increase their visibility and establish credibility with both clients and candidates. Their existing online presence wasn't converting visitors into leads, and they needed a way to:

  • ✓Clearly communicate their service offerings to employers
  • ✓Attract quality blue-collar candidates
  • ✓Display transparent pricing and service tiers
  • ✓Make it easy for both audiences to get in touch
  • ✓Look professional and trustworthy

Scope & Tech Stack

We delivered a complete website solution that included:

  • ✓Custom responsive web design
  • ✓Clear service tier presentation
  • ✓Staff request forms for employers
  • ✓Candidate application pathways
  • ✓Industry-specific content targeting
